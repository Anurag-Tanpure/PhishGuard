    Frequently Asked Questions

    Are my uploaded files stored on your servers?

    No. PhishGuard operates on a zero-retention policy for free-tier users. Payloads are processed in volatile memory (RAM) and wiped immediately after.

    What file sizes are supported?

    Currently, images are limited to 50MB and video files to 100MB to ensure rapid scanning times. Enterprise users have expanded limits via API.
